Basic info about the Dell Inspiron 3535

  • Day Recieved: December 19, 2024
  • Day opened: December 20, 2024
  • Status: Old Machine (I gave it to my aunt)
  • Date Retired: June 10, 2025, July 28, 2025

Specs

These are the specs for the Dell Inspiron 3535

  • CPU: AMD Ryzen 7 7730U
  • RAM: 32GB (originally came with 16GB) DDR4
  • Storage: 512GB Samsung M.2 NVME SSD (no DRAM :( so uhh yeah)
  • Graphics: Integrated Graphics :(
  • Operating System: Windows 11 24H2

History

I got the Dell Inspiron 3535 on December 19, 2024, 1 day later (December 20, 2024) I recorded an unboxing video (which came out 5 days later on December 25, 2025), the laptop has gone through a RAM Upgrade going from 16GB to 32GB, however, this laptop is kind of a temporary laptop, as it's estimated to be retired in June - July 2025.


On June 10, 2025, I retired the Dell Inspiron 3535 and went back to the Lenovo Ideapad Flex 14API


On July 3, 2025, the Lenovo Ideapad Flex 14API broke and I had to switch back to the Inspiron 3535, and then on July 28, I finally gave it away to my aunt.

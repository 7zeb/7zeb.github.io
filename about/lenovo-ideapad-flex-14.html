History

2020-2021

On May 4, 2020, this laptop showed up at my house, and I unboxed it the same day (no videos are left as I never recorded it), this was my main machine until January 4, 2021 when the hinges broke. As a temporary replacment I switched to the Early 2008 Macbook Pro.


May 2021 - March 2024 (Revived State)

After getting the laptop fixed I used it as my main machine until March 2024, when the charger port broke, after that I had to switch back to the Acer Aspire E15.


August 2024 - December 2024 (Days Numbered Part 1)

In August 2024, I got the charger port fixed, and used the laptop as my main laptop until December 20, 2024, when I got the Dell Inspiron 3535.


June 10, 2025 - August 2025 (Days Numbered Part 2)

This is the current state of the laptop, using it as a temporary laptop until I replace it with a desktop PC in August.

My thoughts on the iPhone 16

Written by: 7Zeb (September 17, 2024)


The iPhone 16, Apple's newest iPhone has some great features, and I think (as an Andriod user) that now there is no excuse not to buy an iPhone, why? Because a couple of new features, here's a list.

  1. RCS Support has been added
  2. Camera Button (I wish my Samsung had this)
  3. Action Button on basic model
  4. Apple now allows emulators (not specific to the iPhone 16, but introduced this year)
  5. You can run Windows on your iPhone now!

These are just a few of the things that make the iPhone a great choice if you want a new phone.

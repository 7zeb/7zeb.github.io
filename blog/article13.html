Written on: August 20, 2025

Website updates

The 7Zeb website got updated to NewUI 3, which is a HUGE UI overhaul from NewUI 2.


Views

The total amount of views that the channel got during the Summer Upload Boom is 31,262. Which is a great amount, but far less then I expected sadly The channel did gain 307 Watch Hours, and the channel gained 58 Subscribers.
